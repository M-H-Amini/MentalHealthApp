How to Use the App

Tutorial

Press the "Start" button to begin tracking your workday in terms of focus and sentiment.

Set one or more goals for your day and press "Submit Goal" to receive a motivational picture and quote to help you start strong!

During the day, you can journal and write down whatever is on your mind. Press the "Add Entry" button to add your thoughts to your journal.

If you get distracted during your workday, the app will help you get back on track with guided breathing and focus exercises.

Visit the "Report" tab to view your daily report!
